DESTINY 2

Destiny 2 is expected to be a beautiful visual feast, likely to push the previous Destiny graphics requirements forward. You will need a modern PC to get Destiny 2 running ok.Currently the anticipated minimum system requirements for Destiny 2 is a mid level CPU, like an Intel i5-2400, from around 4 years ago, coupled with a GTX 560 or an AMD R7 260. Destiny 2 system memory requirements are going to be about 6GB RAM minimum. We will update here as more info becomes available.
